FLIP: Online RNA-seq alignment

FLIP is a super light-weight, simple cloud based alignment pipeline that allows easy and fast FASTQ upload and alignment against human and mouse genomes. The pipeline is compatible with the ARCHS4 transcript quantification. Get your transcript quantification without delay. File sizes are currently limited to 5 GB per file.
